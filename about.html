My Fitness Journey

From 92 kg to 68 kg — this transformation rebuilt my mindset, discipline, and lifestyle.

There was a time when I struggled with my weight, energy, and confidence. At one point, I reached 92 kg, and I was constantly uncomfortable with my lifestyle and appearance. Motapa, low energy, confidence issues — everything was at its peak.

On 24 June 2024, I decided to change my life forever. I stepped into the gym for the first time with ZERO knowledge about diet, workouts, consistency, or time management — but with one strong decision:

“No excuses. Bas start karna hai.”

Since that day, I have never looked back. Today, I proudly stand at 68 kg, with a lean, muscular physique, better strength, higher stamina, and unshakeable confidence. This transformation isn't just physical — it has rebuilt my mindset, discipline, and lifestyle.
