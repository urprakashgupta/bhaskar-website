No starvation • No fancy imported food

Sample Day (Veg Diet)

Example only — actual plan will be customized based on your weight, goals & lifestyle.

  • Morning: 1 apple + soaked moong (30g) + water.
  • Breakfast: 2 medium rotis + 100g rajma/white chana/black chana/matar (low oil, home style).
  • Lunch: 2 rotis / 100g rice + 40g soya chunks curry + salad.
  • Evening: 50g roasted chana + green tea / lemon water.
  • Dinner: 1–2 rotis + 30g soya chunks + light sabzi.

Focus always on protein, fibre & portion control. We balance calories scientifically — but plan simple rakhenge.
